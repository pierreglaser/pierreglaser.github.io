Welcome to my personal website! Here are a couple facts about me:


  • I am a final-year PhD Student in the Gatsby Computational Neuroscience Unit under the supervision of Arthur Gretton. I work on designing rigorous training and evaluation methods for probabilistic models applied to scientific data.

  • Previously, I was a software engineer at INRIA, where I worked on improving the performance of distributed scientific computing in Python. Since then, I maintain open sources libraries of the PyData stack. I regularily perform outreach to introduce researchers with Python libraries that could allow them to scale up their experiments, and increase the efficiency of the computational component of their research workflows.
